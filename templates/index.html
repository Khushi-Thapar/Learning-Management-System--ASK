Our Workflow Process

We follow a systematic process that is divided into four phases namely Mobilization and Selection, Training and Assessmnet, Placement and then Follow-up. Continuous reporting is done to the sponsoring organization at all the stages.

Regional Centre For Entrepreneurship Development - RCED (Empowering Youth Since 1995)

RCED is committed to promote skill and entrepreneurship development in Northern states. Our vision is to become an acknowledged pioneer resources institute engaged in promotion of skill and entrepreneurship training. Our mission is to become a catalyst in facilitating emergence of competent first generation entrepreneurs and ensure the availability of skilled manpower.
