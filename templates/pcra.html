About PDOT

Under PCRA, we organize sponsored Awareness Programs and Training Workshops for farmers, youth, rural women and truck operators to raise awareness on the optimum use of fuel. Petroleum Research Conservation Association is a government agency established under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas. The aim of the organization is to spread awareness on how energy can be conserved and its use be limited thereby reducing the overall dependence on petroleum products. It offers policy making support to the Government in terms of formulating strategies by suggesting measures for petroleum conservation. PCRA also promotes research and development activities for devising environment friendly products thereby fostering energy efficiency and sustainable development. For furthering its vision and mission, RCED conducts the following activities on behalf of PCRA:

  • Training and Capacity Building Programs, Seminars and Workshops in various institutes and industries.
  • Driver Training Programs, Transport Workshop and Training for Driver Training Instructors.
  • Kisan Melas, Agriculture Workshops for Farmers and Educational Programs with Krishi Vigyan Kendras.
  • Institutional Awareness Programs with Mahila Mandals, Youth Forums, Residents Welfare Associations.
  • Field Exhibitions for awareness of the general public.
