About KVIC

We have been associated with the Khadi and Village Industries Commission right since RCED’s inception in 1995, conducting Training and Orientation Programs, Bankers’ Meetings and Awareness Programs sponsored by the statutory body. Khadi Village Industries Commission has been established under an act of Parliament for improving livelihood in India especially among the rural community through self employment stemming from the development of village and cottage industries. As a part of this initiative, it also functions as a nodal agency for the promotion of Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) where financial assistance in the form of credit linked subsidy is provided for setting up of micro enterprises. Being an empanelled training partner, RCED organizes the following programs for officials of the implementing partners of the PMEGP scheme viz. KVIC, KVIB, RSETIs, DIC and banks:

  • 1. Training and Orientation Programs for discussing the new initiatives taken by the Government and KVIC to promote the PMEGP.
  • 2. Bankers Meetings to assess and review the progress of PMEGP cases.
  • 3. Awareness Programs with respect to the PMEGP scheme for existing and prospective entrepreneurs as well as other beneficiaries.
  • 4. Potter Training Programs for apprising the potters of this scheme, its limits, legal formalities as well as how they can benefit from it.
